Measuring Ethnic Spatial Segregation

In this paper I propose a new measurement for ethnic diversity by calculating a new segregation index. Unlike other similar segregation index that use administrative units as proxies, I take geographical proximity into account to prevent potential gerrymandering. The new measurement also minimize the effect of "checkerboard problem." Finally I measure the segregation index of Belgium.
